About

We were born out of the belief that every word counts, every color speaks and every idea signifies. For us, it’s essential to understand that every brand - big or small, new or established - carries its own potential to make impact.

By pursuing a good collaboration of culture, data, design and storytelling, we’re here to empower your brand, discover its soul and bring it alive.

We create stories that matter and conversations that stay. For your brand, and for you.

What
We Do?

Milli is a full-service, multidisciplinary creative studio based in Jakarta, Indonesia.

We are a team of impact catalysts who work together in agility, artistry and passion to chase after our mission - making brands alive through three key elements; original concepts, powerful emotions and creative solutions.

We create and redefine brand experience through five core services that we take pride in: Creative & Design, Brand Strategy, Digital & Offline Activation, Media Buying & PR Consultancy and Event Strategy & Management.

What sets us apart is that the founders and creators of the business met as friends. This reflects our value that authenticity and connection are at the heart of everything we do and deliver.
